People involved with your business

The SBA may refer to these people as Individual Contributors. You will not be able to submit your application to the SBA until all everyone has submitted info.

Add person

Who do I include?

  • Firm owner: This is you.
  • Disadvantged Individuals: If you do not own more than 50% of your business, you must provide information on all other disadvantaged individuals to prove 51% ownership by Disadvantaged Individuals.
  • Spouses of Disadvantaged Individuals: If you or any Disadvantaged Individual are married or legally separated, the spouse must provide financial information so the SBA can assess disadvantage.
  • Non-disadvantaged Individuals: This includes:
    • Anyone who owns 10% or more
    • Directors
    • Management Members
    • Partners
    • Officers
