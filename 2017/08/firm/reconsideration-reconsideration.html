Request for reconsideration

Please attach all items listed in your decline letter under “Steps for Reconsideration,” plus any other information that shows your firm’s eligibility for the 8(a) program.

You must provide this information within 45 days of the date of your decline letter. See your decline letter under the Messages tab of your 8(a) Initial Application.
